# Teacher Guide

Teachers use Learn2Master as a decision-support system.

Teacher workflow:

1. Open `/teacher` for class mastery and weak-concept summary.
2. Open `/teacher/learners` to inspect learner profiles.
3. Use `/teacher/portfolio/<learner_id>` to review evidence per learning outcome.
4. Review AI recommendations in `/teacher/ai-insights`.
5. Approve, override, reopen or assign remediation from the learner portfolio.
6. Review practical evidence in `/teacher/practical-evidence`.
7. Add rubric level, score and comments during practical evidence review.
8. Use `/teacher/reports` and `/research/reports` for printable evidence.

Teacher decisions are stored in `teacher_mastery_reviews`, `teacher_interventions` and `teacher_feedback`.
