# Demonstration Script

1. Login as student.
2. Open Student Dashboard.
3. Open Subjects.
4. Select Physics or ICT.
5. Open Mastery Pathway.
6. Attempt Pre-test.
7. Review adaptive notes and videos.
8. Attempt adaptive practice.
9. Complete reflection/evidence step if shown.
10. Attempt Post-test.
11. Confirm mastery status and next LO unlock.
12. Login as teacher.
13. Open Teacher Dashboard, Mastery Monitor and AI Insights.
14. Login as admin.
15. Demonstrate users, schools, curriculum, competencies, reports and logs.
