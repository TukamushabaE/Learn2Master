1. Start the app.
2. Log in as `superadmin / 12345`.
3. Show `/admin/settings` to demonstrate AI personalization, CBC mastery, assessment, privacy and intervention controls.
4. Show `/admin/curriculum` to demonstrate CBC subject, topic, outcome, indicator and success-criteria management.
5. Show `/admin/users` and `/admin/roles` to demonstrate standard role governance.
6. Log in as `elijah / 12345`.
7. Open ICT or Physics from `Subjects`.
8. Open the first learning outcome and take the pre-test.
9. Show adaptive notes, videos, worked examples, activities and AI recommendation.
10. Submit reflection and practical evidence where required.
11. Complete adaptive practice and then post-test after unlock.
12. Open `/learner/ai-coach`, `/ai/explanations` and `/learner/portfolio`.
13. Log in as `teacher / 12345`.
14. Open `/teacher`, `/teacher/learners` and a learner portfolio.
15. Review evidence, approve/remediate mastery and review practical evidence with rubric level.
16. Open `/research/dashboard` and `/research/reports`.
17. Export CSV to show dissertation evaluation indicators.
