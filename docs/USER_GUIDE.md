# Learner Guide

1. Log in as a learner.
2. Open `Subjects` and choose ICT or Physics.
3. Select a course/topic pathway.
4. Open the first unlocked learning outcome.
5. Take the diagnostic pre-test.
6. Review the adaptive notes, videos, worked examples and activities recommended by the system.
7. Submit practical/project evidence where required.
8. Submit the CBC reflection.
9. Complete adaptive practice until weak concepts are resolved.
10. Take the post-test after it unlocks.
11. Review AI feedback, mastery status and the competency portfolio.

- `/student/dashboard`
