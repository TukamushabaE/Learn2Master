Physics is the natural science that studies matter, its fundamental constituents, its motion and behavior through space and time, and the related entities of energy and force.
In the Uganda CBC framework, Physics at the O-Level focuses on understanding the physical world through observation and experimentation.
Key topics include Mechanics, Heat, Light, Sound, Electricity, and Magnetism.
Mechanics covers motion, forces, energy, and power.
Newton's Laws of Motion are fundamental to classical mechanics.
